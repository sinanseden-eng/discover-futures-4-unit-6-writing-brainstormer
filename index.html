Think and Plan

1. The First Look

Imagine you are standing outside the room. Open the door and walk in. What do you see inside? What furniture and objects do you see?

2. Special Objects

Are there any objects that are special in the room? Why are they special? How are they used?

3. The Scent

How does the room smell? If it's the kitchen, for example, can you smell food cooking?

4. Sounds & Action

What noises do you hear in the room? Are there people in the room? What are they doing?

5. Memories & Atmosphere

Think of any happy memories you associate with the room. How does being in the room make you feel?
